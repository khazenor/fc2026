# How to use farm and charm cooking pot:
- place campfire

- right click the campfire to setup the cooking pot

- right click the cooking pot to access its gui

- put container for the recipe (e.g. bowl for yeast) into the container slock

- put in ingredients into the ingredient slots


# How to use bakery small cooking pot:
- place campfire

- shift right click the campfire to setup the cooking pot

- right click the cooking pot to access its gui


# How to use bakery sink:
- place sink

- right click with empty hand to fill sink

- right click with empty bucket to fill bucket


# How to use the farm and charm mincer:
- place the mincer

- right click the mincer if your input ingredient

- hold right click on the mincer to process the ingredient to their output


# How to use the farm and charm crafting bowl:
- place the crafting bowl

- right click the ingredients into the crafting bowl

- (you can shift right click the bowl to remove ingredients)

- after placing all ingredients right click the bowl, hold right click the crafting bowl 
with an empty hand to craft
- after crafting id done, right click with empty hand again to extract the outputs


# How to use the farm and charm stove:
- place the stove

- put in fuel (e.g. coal) into the fuel slot

- put in ingredients into the ingredient slots

# How to use the Caking station:
- Right click the caking station while holding a cake dough. After that you can do different interactions with the cake dough: 

- Knife -> Cutting -> Use Jam on it -> Make Cupcakes 
- Knife -> Rolling Pin -> Use Jam on it -> Make Cookies 
- Using Jam on it -> Make a cake

# How to use the roaster from farm and charm:
- place the roaster on top of a stove

# How to get tea leaves from herbal brews:
- grow green tea leaves by planting tea blossoms on farmlands or in botany pots

- craft a green tea leaf block in a crafting table

- place the green tea leaf block on the ground and wait for it to dry

- break the block to get green tea leaves after green particles no longer emit from the tea 
block
## black tea
- craft dried green tea leaf block from green tea leaves

- place the block down to dry to a black tea leave block

## Oolong tea
- craft a mix tea leaf block using green and black tea leaves

- place the block down to dry to a oolong tea leaf block


# How to brew teas from herbal brews:
- place a stove

- place the tea kettle on top of the stove

- place the ingredients and the container in their respective slots

- add blaze powder into the blaze powder slot until the pot it hot enough to brew the tea.

- (you can grow blaze rods using blaze powder in the botany pot)


# How to make grape juice from vinery:
- place a grape pot

- right click the pot with grapes until you can't anymore

- jump on top of the pot until the content of the pot is fully liquid

- right click the pot with empty wine bottle to get the juice


# How to brew wine from vinery:
- place down a fermentation barrel

- place the correct juice on the top left slot (you can see which juice is needed in jei by 
hovering slightly right of the coil)

- place the ingredients in the bottom three slots


# How to dry grains using a silo:
- To get dried corn, wheat, or barley you need to use a silo.

- The silo connects in a 1x1, 2x2, or 3x3 grid and can be extended up to a height of 9 blocks

- Make sure both of your hands are empty, and right click on the hatch of the silo to open 
it.
- After that right click the hatch of the silo with corn, wheat, or barley.

- After a while the dried goods will pop out of the sile if the hatch is still open.  Or if 
you have closed the hatch the good will pop out after you open the hatch again.

# How to brew beer using the brewing station:
- To start brewing, place down the brewing station.

- Put the ingredients you looked up in JEI in to the basket by right clicking them in.

- After that put in some water by right clicking with a water bucket.

- Right click the stove with a coal to fire up the brewing machine


## BREWING PROCESS
- During the brewing process, you need to monitor the brewing station.

- If the temperature gauge is red, you need to cool it down with a bucket of water

- If the chimney is smoking, you need to add more coal to the stove.

- If the water runs out, you need to add more with a water bucket.

- If the water runs over, you need to remove some water with an empty bucket.

# How to make monster part lasts with ash
- You can make ash by smelting grains (e.g. wheat)
- Ash can then be used to duplicate monster parts in the crafting table.

# How to make furniture using the stonecutter
- In this modpack you can convert logs into other furniture by putting them in the stonecutter

- After a furniture is cut, it can then be converted again into other furniture, or event back to logs

# Catching Fishes
- This modpack has the Tide mod installed and there is a mini game for fish catches.
- Hold right click with a fishing rod to wind up your fishing rod
- Releasing right click will cast the rod
- When a fish bites, a moving target appears.
- Right click again with the target as close to the center as possible.
- The closer to the center, the higher chance you will actually catch the fish.

# Taking Photos with the Camera
- To start taking photos, right click with the camera with paper in your inventory
- after that, left click to take a photo
- scroll to zoom
- to place a photo shift right click a wall
- shift right click a photo to resize and configure (hold shift to shrink)
- shift right click an album to collect photos
